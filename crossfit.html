Crossfit

Crossfit, which many consider to be a sport, is actually a "program" of strengthening and general physical conditioning of the fitness and wellness type. Focused on a series of functional movements in continuous evolution, its main feature is the high intensity of execution (HIT - High Intensity Training).

The cardinal principle of crossfit training is the variety of stimuli. The name itself says it, composed of the words "cross" and "fit", that is the intertwining of several motor activities. A good crossfitter knows how to perform many strengthening exercises, bodyweight and with overloads (kettlebell, barbell, etc.), but also aerobic or mixed disciplines (running, rope jumping, rowing, swimming, cycling, etc.).
Four training phases:

  • Warm up
  • Skill/Strenght
  • Workout of the day
  • Recovery

The crossfit is particurarly indicated for the high energy consume, to get a better blood pressure and a better metabolism.
