"Gymtastic: how to get the best shape of your life"

The bodyweight training is able to involve multiple muscles simultaneously.
Starting from low intensity excercises, using our schedules you will able to see good results after just few weeks and you will learn new skills and new progressions.

Our exercises have the main focus on the three main areas of the body: upper body, core and lower body.
Dedicating two days a week for each area for just few minutes every time you will be able to tone up your body and gain strenght and energy.

Applying meditation and eating healty food you can increase your gains in the best way possible.
Our nutritionist will help you providing an individual schedule, to maximize the results and reduce your body's fat percentage.
